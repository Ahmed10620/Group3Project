Job Description 1: SOC Analyst

Job Location: Doha, Qatar

Position Title: SOC Analyst

Brief Description: The SOC Analyst is responsible for first-line security monitoring, alert triage, and escalation of cybersecurity incidents across ORA Technology's client environments.

Salary Range: QAR 7,000 to QAR 22,000 per month

Reports To: SOC Team Lead (Qatar Branch)

Key Responsibilities

  1. Monitor SIEM dashboards for potential threats.
  2. Perform initial analysis and prioritisation of alerts.
  3. Document incidents and escalate when necessary.
  4. Assist in vulnerability scanning and basic security checks.
  5. Prepare clear incident summaries and reports.

Required Qualifications & Skills

Essential

  • Diploma or degree in Cybersecurity, IT, or related field.
  • Understanding of SIEM platforms.
  • Knowledge of common attack types and networking basics.
  • Strong communication and analytical skills.

Preferable

  • CompTIA Security+ certification.
  • Knowledge of MITRE ATT&CK.
  • Some IT support or cybersecurity experience.

Job Description 2: Data Analyst

Job Location: Doha, Qatar

Job Title: Data Analyst

Brief Description: The Data Analyst will join ORA Technologies' Qatar team, analysing and visualising business data while supporting decision-making processes across regional operations.

Salary Range: 9,000 - 32,000 QAR per month

Reports To: Data Analytics Manager (Australia HQ with Qatar oversight)

Key Responsibilities

  1. Collect, clean, and structure datasets.
  2. Identify trends through statistical analysis.
  3. Create dashboards using Power BI or Tableau.
  4. Collaborate on data-driven business improvements.
  5. Present findings to Qatar and Australia-based teams.

Required Qualifications & Skills

Essential

  • Degree in Data Analytics, Statistics, IT, or related field.
  • Proficiency in SQL and Python or R.
  • Experience with Power BI, Tableau, or similar tools.
  • Strong analytical and communication skills.

Preferable

  • Experience with cloud platforms (Azure, AWS, GCP).
  • Knowledge of basic machine learning techniques.
  • Relevant data certifications.
